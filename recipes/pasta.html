Recipe For White Sauce Pasta

Description About White Sauce Pasta

White Sauce Pasta is a creamy and delicious dish made with a rich, cheesy white sauce. It is packed with flavors from herbs, sautéed vegetables, and perfectly cooked pasta, making it a comforting meal.

Materials Required for White Sauce Pasta:

  • 2 cups pasta (penne, fusilli, or macaroni)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (mozzarella or processed cheese)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 1/2 cup boiled sweet corn
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • Salt to taste

Steps to Make White Sauce Pasta:

  1. Boil the Pasta – Cook the pasta in boiling salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the White Sauce – In a pan, melt butter over low heat. Add all-purpose flour and cook for 1-2 minutes until light golden.
  3. Add Milk – Slowly pour in warm milk while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Add Cheese – Mix in grated cheese and let it melt into the sauce.
  5. Season the Sauce – Add salt, black pepper, oregano, and chili flakes for seasoning.
  6. Sauté Vegetables – In another pan, sauté onions, bell peppers, and sweet corn in a little butter.
  7. Combine Everything – Add the cooked pasta and sautéed vegetables into the white sauce. Mix well.
  8. Serve Hot – Garnish with extra cheese and herbs, then serve warm!
