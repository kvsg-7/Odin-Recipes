Recipe For Corn Pizza

Description About Corn Pizza

Corn pizza is a delicious variation of traditional pizza that features sweet and juicy corn kernels as a topping. The natural sweetness of the corn complements the rich flavors of melted cheese, tangy tomato sauce, and a crispy or soft pizza crust.

Materials Required for Corn Pizza:

  • Pizza base
  • Sweet corn kernels
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Bell peppers (optional, sliced)
  • Onions
  • Oregano
  • Chili flakes
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Black pepper
  • Mayonnaise or white sauce (optional)

Steps to Make Corn Pizza:

  1. Preheat the Oven – Set your oven to 200°C (392°F) and let it preheat for 10 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Pizza Base – Place the pizza base on a baking tray or pizza stone. Lightly brush it with butter or olive oil for extra crispiness.
  3. Spread the Sauce – Evenly spread a generous layer of pizza sauce over the base.
  4. Add Cheese – Sprinkle grated mozzarella cheese to cover the base.
  5. Add Toppings – Spread boiled sweet corn evenly. You can also add bell peppers, onions, or other veggies if desired.
  6. Season It – Sprinkle oregano, chili flakes, and black pepper for extra flavor.
  7. Bake the Pizza – Place it in the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes or until the cheese melts and turns golden brown.
  8. Serve & Enjoy – Remove the pizza, let it cool slightly, slice it, and serve hot with ketchup or a dip!
